Section 05
Logo Usage

Protecting the wordmark keeps the brand strong. Respect the minimum clear space and size, and never alter the mark. When in doubt, reach for an approved file.

Clear Space

Keep clear space around the wordmark at least equal to the cap-height of the letters, free of other type, imagery and graphics.

Minimum Size

Digital · 130px wide min
Emblem · 24px min

Do not reproduce the wordmark below 28 mm / 130 px wide. For small spaces, use the emblem on its own.

✓Reverse on dark
